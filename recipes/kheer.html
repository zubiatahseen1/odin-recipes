The ever so delicious Kaddu Ki Kheer

5-6 Servings 350 KCals
Prep time:4 Hours Cook time:1 Hour Total time:5 Hours

Ingredients:

  • 2 litres full cream milk
  • 1 cup bottlegourd/lauki/doodhi peeled and grated
  • 3 tbsp sabudana/sago- soaked for 4-5 hours
  • 3 tbsp basmati rice soaked for 1 hr, drained, dried and coarsely powdered
  • 3 tbsp cashewnut paste 10 -12 cashews blanched and made into paste
  • 50 gms khoya mix it with some milk without lumps
  • 3 tbsp milkmaid/condensed milk
  • 1 cup sugar/ as required
  • ¾ tsp cardamom powder
  • ¼ tsp green food color
  • few drops of vanilla essence(optional)
  • 3 tbsp blanched dry fruits finely sliced almonds, pistachios, cashews for garnishing

Procedure:

Cook the kaddu:

  1. Firstly, in a pan, add grated kaddu/bottlegourd.
  2. Into it, add 1 cup water and cook the kaddu until all the water is absorbed and until it gets soft.

Making of kheer:

  1. In a separate non stick pan/wok, add milk and bring it to a boil.
  2. Stir it for 5-10 minutes on low flame.
  3. Add cooked bottlegourd/kaddu into the milk.
  4. Stir and cook for 5 minutes on low flame.
  5. Add soaked sago/sabudana, mix and keep stirring and cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Add basmati rice flour, mix, keep stirring for another 5 minutes.
  7. Add cashewnut paste, mix and stir for 5 minutes.
  8. Add milkmaid and mix it well.
  9. Add khoya mixed with milk, mix and cook by stirring for few minutes.
  10. Add sugar as per requirement, mix and cook for another 5 minutes.
  11. Add cardamom powder, green food color, mix well and stir well.
  12. Cook for 5 minutes on low flame.
  13. Chill the dessert in refrigerator for few hours.
  14. Garnish with sliced dry fruits and serve.
